About the Author

A shley Keller Little is a self-made, multi award-winning entrepreneur for over 10 years. She has been featured in numerous magazines and publications. In 2019, she received the prestigious Woman of The Year award for Washington County, Maryland, while also receiveing several proclamations from the Governor (MD), Senators, and delegates for her dedication and advocacy for women in business and women’s rights.
She is an artist and designer and uses her platform to influence others to be positive, strong, fearless, and unapologetically themselves. She has also gained a following regarding her spiritual truth teachings which help shed light into the darkness of many taboo subjects. She will cover many of these topics and more in future books, on social media, and on her up and coming podcast with her devout husband Chis. Ashley has been with her husband for 14 years. They have two amazing children, Brooklyn Jet and Bronx, which they adore.
Ashley has truly overcome the darkness and evil that has so desperately tried to prevail. She has come out on top by the grace of God and with his favor. He has truly made her fearless to lead others to the truth and to stand strong against the enemy.
